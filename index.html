This is the daily agenda for What Did the 2010’s Look Like?, a Spring 2020 workshop at Parsons School of Design.

Syllabus, Homework Drop

Week 2 – January 31

In Class
  • Discuss readings
  • Editorial Examples
  • Modules & Design Library Activities
  • Individual and small group meetings
At Home
  • Pick one of your texts to use as your written entry. Using our Sketch library and type system, design three layouts for your content. Please have 3 different layouts to share next week, as well as a draft of your visual components saved out as either jpgs or gifs.
  • Design a module that fits within our system. Use it in of your layout sketches and save it on a separate artboard with a brief description on how you imagine it working.
  • Read pages 2 through 21 and 131 through 133 from Several Short Sentences About Writing – Verlyn Klinkenborg. Consider these points and apply it to your drafts.
  • Upload your work to Sketch cloud, update the sharing settings and have it ready to review in class. Also save a plain text version of your text in the google drive.

Week 1 – January 24

In Class
  • Faculty Introductions
  • Student Introductions
  • Project brief & ice breaker activities
At Home
  • Think through three different ideas for your entry and write the first 2 sentences of each one. For each one, find or create a supporting graphic that could inform a visual language.
  • Read Profile Page – Florian Freier and The Drive Home – Emma Cline. Write a short response that compares the two.
  • Save both assignments in the homework drop.
